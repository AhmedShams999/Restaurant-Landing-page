Our Story

Four Decades of Authentic Egyptian Flavors

Founded in 1985 by the Hassan family, Nile Heritage Restaurant has been serving authentic Egyptian cuisine in the heart of Fayoum for over four decades. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the rich culinary traditions of ancient Egypt.

We source our ingredients directly from local farmers in the fertile Fayoum Oasis, ensuring every dish captures the true essence of Egyptian flavors. Our restaurant overlooks the magnificent Lake Qarun, providing our guests with an unforgettable dining experience that combines exceptional food with breathtaking natural beauty.
